How Health R.A.I.S. Regisiters Phones

Registering

The platform will generate authorization codes specific to each hospital or clinic.

To register a cell phone for a specific healthcare facility, text that facility’s authorization code to (571) 200-4949

Reporting

To make a report, format texts as follows:
(name of disease) , (number of cases)

To include multiple reports in one text, separate each with a semicolon (;)

Alerts

To generate an alert, simply enter into the HealthRAIS platform and type up your preferred message.

Frequently used alerts will be saved within the platform for ease of selection later.




Information Network

Health-R.A.I.S. facilitate two-way information sharing between the user and the local healthcare providers sending data. Users can decide what messages should be texted back to the health care locations that each data set is assigned to. This can be tailored to warnings of an outbreak, vigilance of a certain symptoms, or notifications of incoming supplies or international help. The User will have full control over the area that will be messaged the user's custom alert or notification. This added benefit will help fix the disconnect between the people that make resource decisions and the people on the front lines of any outbreak.

Hard to Reach Places

The platform utilizes a Django server intended to live on site with the user and the Twilio API to facilitate SMS data collection. The Django server will collect and aggregate the data it receives from hospitals, clinics, and NGOs reporting health data. The server will constantly analyze the data to look for areas that have abnormal levels of reports and issue warnings in a front-end interface that the client will easily be able to interact with. At the user’s discretion, all registered phone numbers in an area can be contacted with an alert, customizable by the user. Additional datasets can be called from Sailfish to try and predict warning areas where infectious diseases have a higher risk of spreading. Finally, access to the server will be granted based on client specification, to either increase the transparency of all collected data or to restrict access to prevent possible panic.
